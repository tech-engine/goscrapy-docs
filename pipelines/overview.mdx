title: "Overview"
description: "Understanding Pipelines in GoScrapy"
---

Pipelines are responsible for processing and exporting the items yielded by your spiders.

When you call `s.Yield(record)`, the engine pushes the record into the Pipeline Manager, which passes it through all configured pipelines sequentially.

Pipelines are typically used for:

- Cleansing HTML data.
- Validating scraped data (checking that the items contain certain fields).
- Checking for duplicates (and dropping them).
- Storing the scraped item in a database, file, or cloud storage.
